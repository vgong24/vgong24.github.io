This app will require an email or google+ account for authentication and for creating a personal account. You can create a username that would be shared publically in the app when hosting an Event activity or participating in an event as well as messaging in chat rooms.

Your location will be shared with other users when you are attending an event to notify other members of the event on where you are in relation to where the event will take place. Your location will only be tracked depending on the start time of the event as well as you approach near the event location.

Your device id would be used to transmit push notifications as well as send messages to other users through the chat room.
